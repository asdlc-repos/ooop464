# Overview

An internal tool for WSO2 employees to request time off and for managers to review and approve those requests.

# Personas

- Employee — requests leave and checks their available balance.
- Manager — reviews and approves or denies leave requests from their team.

# Features

- Employees submit leave requests by selecting dates and choosing a leave type.
- Managers see requests from their direct reports and can approve or reject them.
- Everyone can view their remaining leave balance for each type.
- Employees see the status of their submitted requests.
- Managers can view their team's upcoming approved leave on a calendar.
- Leave notifications
